Welcome to Gin Rummy Scoresheet!

This app is meant to be used as a handy score tracker for offline Gin Rummy games.

Create matches against your friends, log games, and see your win rates all in one convenient place.

Matches

Browse current and past matches, and see scores and outcomes.

Delete matches you're no longer interested in playing.

Add new matches against any player in the system.

Access existing matches to update wins, losses, and points.

Games

Add completed games to matches and watch your score increase.

Edit or delete past games that were mistakenly entered.

Players

View all players, along with the share of matches and games they've won.

Access basic profile details for any player.

Edit your own profile details, including your username and contact information.
